Hello, my name is PHP

I am a general purpose scripting language geared towards web development. PHP is an acronym, its expanded form is PHP: Hypertext Preprocessor. I am very popular in industry despite of what some misinformed developers have to say about me. You will learn about me in the next course i.e. Web Programming Studio (COSC3046). One more thing- I live on the server-side and browsers cannot understand my syntax.
